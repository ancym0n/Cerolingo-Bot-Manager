FAQ

1. What is CerolingoBM?

CerolingoBM stands for Cerolingo Bot Manager. It is a tool designed to automate the process of creating Duolingo accounts and managing them, such as completing lessons and accumulating XP.

2. What are its main features?

CerolingoBM can create and manage multiple Duolingo accounts. It also allows you to automatically maintain streaks or work your way up the leaderboards. Additionally, the tool can be customized to match personal preferences.

3. Which operating systems are supported?

CerolingoBM is currently developed for Windows due to its reliance on running .exe files. Support for other operating systems may be added in the future, but there is no set timeline.
